Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect information that you provide directly to us. This includes your email address when you create an account, and any financial data you choose to enter such as expense amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We do not collect or store sensitive financial information like credit card numbers, bank account numbers, or passwords.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our services. Specifically, we use your data to: display your expense records and generate summaries; help you understand your spending patterns through category breakdowns and visualizations; and communicate with you about service updates or security notices. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal financial data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on servers located in India. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. You retain full ownership of your data and can export or delete it at any time from your account settings.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly may use third-party services for hosting, analytics, and customer support. These partners have access to limited information necessary to perform their functions but are contractually obligated to maintain confidentiality and security. We do not share your personal financial data with these providers beyond what is required for service operation.

5. Cookies and Tracking

We use essential cookies to maintain your session and preferences. These cookies are necessary for the app to function properly. We do not use advertising cookies or tracking pixels from third-party advertisers.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You can do this directly through your account settings or by contacting us. You may also opt out of non-essential communications at any time.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at support@spendly.com.
